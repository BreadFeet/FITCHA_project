How Fitcha Works For You

Machine Learning

It finds your size using machine learning model based on your height and weight information. The model was built with hundreds of thousands of size data for best prediction.

Connect to Shopping Page

Once it finds your size, we suggest some famous shopping websites where you can shop with your size. It directs you to the page of your size. How easy!

Provide Your Right Size

Was your right size different from what we recommended? If you provide the right size, it can improve our service. It also directs you to shopping websites with the size!
