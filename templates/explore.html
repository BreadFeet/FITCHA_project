Relation between Features

Size seems to have relation mainly with height and weight. The first Age-Height chart tells the older the age and the taller the height, the bigger the size. On the other hand, the second Age-Weight chart demonstrates weight have much impact on size, but not age. The third chart shows obvious impact of height and weight on the size. As both value get bigger, people choose bigger size as we can expect. Thus, height and weight are explicitly relevant features, and age needs analysing to see if it has an effect on prediction of the size. As a result, age was included as one of the features because the result returned better prediction.

See What Others with Your Size Choose

You can explore what size is popularly chosen between people who have similar height and weight with you. Use the slider to narrow down around your values as there might not be the exactly same size with you. Give it some enough range to see what is probable for your size to fall into. If you hover over values, you can see details of each spot. It just gives you a rough idea on how the recommendation was made, but it might not be same with our recommendation.
